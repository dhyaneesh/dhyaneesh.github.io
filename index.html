What I work on

I build LLM pipelines, retrieval systems, and agentic workflows — mostly in Python, mostly for enterprise use cases. At Blend360 I built a persona generation agent that synthesised financial customer personas at scale, a decision intelligence dashboard for automated narrative reporting, and inference workflows running 100,000+ concurrent runs.
